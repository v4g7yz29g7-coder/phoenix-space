# 🐦‍🔥 Phoenix: Roadmap

We are building a living Garden. Here is what's blooming and what we're planting next.

## ✅ What already works

- **Eye of the Phoenix** — a portal to the Academy, speaking 7 languages.
- **Six Gates** — philosophical practices translated into all languages.
- **Workshop** — creating artifacts and writing them to the blockchain.
- **Circle of Companions** — P2P chat, calls, and files directly.
- **Digital Passport (DID)** — sovereign identity without passwords.
- **Architect's Profile** — your reputation, avatar, and collection.
- **Admin Panel** — managing complaints and content.
- **Network of Gardens** — interactive map with world wonders.
- **Constitution** — our principles, available to everyone.

## 🚀 In Progress

- **Network of Gardens (P2P)** — ability to run your own node.
- **Asynchronous messaging** — letters waiting for you up to 7 days.
- **Phnx Tokens** — gratitude economy for contributing to the Garden.

## 🌐 Big Dream

- Decentralized network of Masters without a central server.
- Public registry of artifacts on the blockchain.
- Anyone can open their own Garden and connect with others.

We go step by step, without haste. Thank you for being with us. 🐦‍🔥
